Academic Success Program

Originally, I started out as a tutor in the Ross Academic Center in order to make some money so I could cover some of my college expenses. However, once I started to tutor more and more it became so much more. I genuinely cared about the people I was tutoring and helping them learn the material. As a result, I started committing time to creating study plans and write extra problems. The most rewarding part came when one of the people I was tutoring actually told me that he wanted to be an accountant because of all the work we did together. Grades and internships are temporary, but that kind of impact truly lasts a lifetime.
